## AUTHENTICATION PAGES

### 1.1 Registration Page (`/register`)

**Layout:** Centered single-column form

**Header Section:**
- PetCircle logo (top left)
- Tagline: "Find loving homes for pets in need"
- Navigation link: "Already have an account? Log in"

**Main Form:**
- Page title: "Create Your Account"
- Subtitle: "Join our community of responsible pet lovers"

**Form Fields:**
- First Name (text input, required)
  - Placeholder: "Enter your first name"
  - Error message: "First name is required"
- Last Name (text input, required)
  - Placeholder: "Enter your last name"
  - Error message: "Last name is required"
- Email Address (email input, required)
  - Placeholder: "you@example.com"
  - Error messages: "Email is required" / "Please enter a valid email"
- Phone Number (tel input, required)
  - Placeholder: "+1 (555) 000-0000"
  - Format helper text: "We'll send you a verification code"
- Password (password input, required)
  - Placeholder: "Create a strong password"
  - Show/hide password toggle icon
  - Password strength indicator bar (weak/medium/strong)
  - Requirements list below:
    - Minimum 8 characters
    - At least one uppercase letter
    - At least one number
    - At least one special character
- Confirm Password (password input, required)
  - Placeholder: "Re-enter your password"
  - Error message: "Passwords must match"

**Terms Agreement:**
- Checkbox (required): "I agree to the Terms of Service and Privacy Policy"
- Links to: Terms of Service, Privacy Policy (open in new tab)

**Submit Button:**
- Text: "Create Account"
- Full width, primary color
- Disabled state until all required fields valid
- Loading spinner when submitting

**Footer:**
- Text: "Already have an account? Log in"
- Link to login page

**Success Flow:**
- Redirect to email verification page
- Show success message: "Account created! Please verify your email."

**Example for "Behavioral Issues":**
- Card 1: "Professional Dog Trainers"

  - Link: "Find Trainers in Your Area"
  - Icon: Training whistle
- Card 2: "Free Online Training Resources"
  - Description: "Video tutorials and guides for common issues"
  - Link: "View Resources"
  - Icon: Play button
