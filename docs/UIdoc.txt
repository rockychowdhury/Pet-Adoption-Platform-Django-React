## üéØ Overall Design Philosophy

**Visual Identity:**
- Modern, warm, and approachable aesthetic
- Primary Color: Charcoal (#2D2D2D) for trust and sophistication
- Accent Color: Warm Gold/Brown (#A68A6D) for emotional connection
- Background: Cream (#FFF8E7) for comfort
- Typography: Concert One (headings), Inter (body text)
- Border Radius: Generous (16-24px) for friendly feel
- Shadows: Soft and layered for depth

**Dark Mode (Comfort Mode):**
- Deep Slate (#0F172A) background
- Orange (#FDBA74) and Indigo (#818CF8) accents
- High contrast for readability
- Maintains warmth through color choices

## üé® Global Design Patterns

### **Cards:**
- Always rounded-3xl (24px) or rounded-2xl (16px)
- Subtle border (1px, border color)
- Soft shadow
- Hover: Lift slightly (translateY -4px), increase shadow

### **Buttons:**
- **Primary:** Filled, primary color, white text, bold, rounded-xl, 48px height
- **Secondary:** Outlined, border 2px, accent color, rounded-xl, same height
- **Ghost:** No border, gray text, hover background gray-50
- **Icon:** Circular or square, 40px, icon centered, hover background

### **Forms:**
- Labels: 14px, medium, gray, 4px margin bottom
- Inputs: 48px height, rounded-xl, border, padding 16px horizontal
- Focus: Accent border, ring 2px accent/20
- Error: Red border, red text below

### **Typography Scale:**
- Page Titles: 40-48px, bold, logo font
- Section Headings: 24-32px, bold
- Card Titles: 18-20px, bold
- Body: 16px, regular, line-height 1.5
- Small: 14px
- Tiny: 12px

### **Spacing System:**
- Use multiples of 4px
- Component padding: 16px (small), 24px (medium), 32px (large)
- Section margins: 48px (small), 64px (medium), 80px (large)
- Element gaps: 8px (tight), 16px (normal), 24px (loose), 32px (spacious)

### **Responsive Breakpoints:**
- Mobile: < 768px (single column, full width, stacked)
- Tablet: 768-1024px (2 columns)
- Desktop: > 1024px (multi-column, max-width containers)

### **Animations:**
- Hover: 0.2s ease
- Modal/Drawer: 0.3s ease-out
- Page transitions: 0.4s ease
- Use transform for performance (translateY, scale)
